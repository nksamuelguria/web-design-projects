GSEF
Global Sustainable Education Forum

A forum is required to oversee the implementation and evaluation of E3i model.

The forum will also work for the global partnership under "One World, One Goal" motive to achieve the SDGs through the implementation of E3I model which are associated to the global warming, environment, Water etc.

The forum will work under the leadership and vision of Honourable Prime Minister as the model will be launched by him.

Mission

To work towards attainment of SDGs through E3I model

Objectives

  • To bring all the stakeholders on a single platform and to turn the dream of Sustainable education by 2030 into reality.
  • To solve the larger global problems through collaboration and joint research
  • To help the under-developed and developing country gets the mentoring from the developing countries for development of sustainable education
