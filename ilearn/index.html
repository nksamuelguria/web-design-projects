About iLearn

Launched under the e-VidyaBharti Network Project, iLearn is the brainchild of the third India-Africa summit where India committed to provide scholarships to 15,000 African students and professionals in order to facilitate their education in emerging Indian institutions. It is basically a technological upgrade of the Pan-African e-Network Project (Phase 1) which was executed in 48 partner countries. Built with the aim of the development of African students, it brings to them opportunities to get quality education and acquire the requisite skillset free of cost, sitting at their home countries.

The program is fully funded by the Ministry of External Affairs, Government of India and will deliver certifications, diplomas, undergraduate and postgraduate degree for the next five years in all 54 African nations.

About Chandigarh University

Chandigarh University has always taken pride in being a center of excellence and innovation. It has been awarded the prestigious A+ grade by the National Assessment and Accreditation Council (NAAC). The University is now emerging as one of the most preferred destination for online learning. The Chandigarh University Institute of Distance & Online Learning is aligned with Hon’ble Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi’s Digital India program and reflects its adaptive and innovative approach.

The main aim of IDOL is to offer a new age education and give ample opportunities to online learners. With the iLearn initiative, CU IDOL will now take online learning to African doorsteps, making education more accessible and providing the students with a smooth learning experience.

Benefits of iLearn

Brimming Scholarships for Certificates and Online Degree

All the course fee of the students will be covered by the scholarship provided by MEA under the e-VBAB program. Applicants only need to apply for the scholarship to cover their tuition or examination fees and focus on their learning without any worry.

Ease of Accessibility

The program aims at making education more accessible and provides students with the flexibility of accessing the course material anywhere, anytime. Even for exams, the students do not need to travel anywhere as they have the option to appear for the examination from their homes.

Learner-centric experience

There are a number of courses available under the scheme. The learners can choose to study any course of their choice based on their own accord.

Learn from Innovative Minds

Under iLearn, African nationals will get the opportunity to learn from accomplished academicians who hold excellence in their respective fields.

International Collaborations

The students will get international exposure as they will study with international peers and become a part of our global community. Currently, CU is in partnership with 312 universities like University of North Alabama, USA; University of New Castle, Australia; Christian Brothers University, USA; Okanagan College, Canada; University of Canberra, Australia and many more.
