Who
we are?

Indian Minorities Foundation is conceptualized to amplify the voice of minority communities in the country by bringing together like-minded people and organizations for advocacy, networking, engagement, endorsing and creating equal opportunities, and to build a more equitable and inclusive society, where minorities are empowered to thrive and progress.

With representation from all minority communities, the Foundation aims to promote inclusion, diversity, and equality through dialogue, cooperation, and action, bridging the gap between the minorities and the government entities, and addressing the challenges and intersectional aspects of inequality.

About
Foundation

Indian Minorities Foundation is envisioned to exemplify the spirit of "Sanyukt Bharat, Samridh Bharat" by creating an enabling environment to put minority issues at the heart of public policy and further equality and equity in the country.

The Foundation aims to facilitate and leverage dialogue to empower minorities through strategic national and global partnerships that bring together expertise and vision to drive change.
