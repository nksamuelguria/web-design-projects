Rules & Regulations

  • All Decisions made by the Appointed “Selection Committee” Or "Jury" regarding Selections, Screenings, Awards at the Festival in entirety will be the final decision. In case of Dispute, the decision of the Selection Committee shall be final.
  • A film, series, documentary, music video etc selected for the festival does not run in competition automatically. Selection Committee or a Jury will select the films, series, music video, etc that feature in the Competition.
  • The Festival shall not bear / reimburse any freight or other charges in foreign or Indian currency.
  • Once the film, documentary, music video etc officially selected for screening, or for the awards the film, music video, series etc shall not be withdrawn in any circumstance.
  • Exceptions to the festival's regulations must be authorized by the Festival Director.
  • Festival Management will not be responsible if the film is not screened due to problems with the video, or any error in the link or password shared with us.
  • Multiple Entries can be submitted, but with separate entry forms. No Submission Form Will Be Accepted Without The Appropriate Payment Of The Fee.
  • The festival will not bear any expenses regarding lodging, boarding, traveling etc of the applicant during the film festival event.
  • Submission fee is non refundable.
  • 1Awards and Certificates will be provided only to those who give their physical presence at the venue. For courier, extra charges will be applied, depending upon the location of the courier from Chandigarh University.
  • 1The Festival reserves the right to refuse or cancel the screening of any film if advised by authorized technicians that the film is not of sufficient quality to ensure satisfactory public screening.

If your film is selected

  • Filmmakers will get the official selection notification through email ID and Filmfreeway
  • An email specifying the technical requirements for cinema screening and a link for upload will be sent out. We accept ProRes 422, 422HQ and H.264 file formats and 2-stereo and not DCP formats nor 5.1 surround sound.
  • Selected films may be considered for any award at Film Festival.
  • All film and music rights and clearances must have been obtained by the filmmakers (in accordance with copyright laws)
  • You cannot withdraw your film from the festival after selection by the selection committee.
  • CUIFF is authorized to screen winning films on their media channel for branding purposes.
