About CUIFF 2023

Chandigarh University International Film Festival (CUIFF) is an annual international film festival that has been organized by Chandigarh University since 2015. The festival aims to showcase the best cinema from across the world, featuring both established and emerging filmmakers. while also providing a platform for young filmmakers to showcase their talent.

Over the years, CUIFF has become one of the most highly anticipated film festivals in the region, drawing filmmakers, critics, and cinema enthusiasts from around the world. The festival features a wide range of categories, including Best Short Films, Best Stop Motion, BEst 2D/3D Animation Short Film, Best VFX short Film, Best Showreel and Best Documentary, among others.

One of the highlights of the festival is the presence of renowned faces from the film industry, who attend the festival to interact with audiences and participate in panel discussions. The festival has seen the likes of Bollywood actors and many more among others, attending the festival in past years.

In 2015, the festival was first organized as the International Film Festival Chandigarh (IFFC). However, in 2023, the festival was rebranded as Chandigarh University International Film Festival (CUIFF) to reflect the growing stature of the festival and its association with Chandigarh University.

Over the years, the festival has featured a wide range of films, from short films and documentaries to Showreels. The festival has also provided a platform for emerging filmmakers to showcase their work and connect with industry professionals.

CUIFF has been successful in promoting the art of cinema and bringing together filmmakers from around the world. With its focus on celebrating the diversity of cinema and providing a platform for creative expression, the festival has become a staple of the international film calendar.

CUIFF is now widely regarded as one of the most significant film festivals in the region and has helped put Chandigarh on the map as a hub for cinema. With each passing year, the festival continues to grow in stature, attracting a growing number of filmmakers, critics, diversity, and innovation in the world of film, and continues to attract the best and brightest talent from around the world. CUIFF looks forward to many more successful years of celebrating the art and craft of filmmaking.
