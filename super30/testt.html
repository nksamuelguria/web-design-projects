ChandigarhSuper30 Aim, Mission, Vision

AIM

To help overcome the challenges for building a successful career, Chandigarh Super 30 aims to provide free of cost training to the deserving aspirants of economically weaker sections of Chandigarh to qualify prestigious competitive exams.

Mission

Our mission is to help talented aspirants clear the most prestigious competitive exams in their field of choice. We aspire to uplift the selected aspirants of the economically weaker section of Chandigarh by providing them the facilities to prepare and clear prestigious competitive exams.

Vision

Providing the required mentorship and training to talented aspirants to attain their career goals is the core objective of Super 30 Academy. Our expert faculty prepares aspirants with the right methodologies and curriculum that leads them to qualify the competitive exams in their related fields.

Eligibility

Entrance Exam: NEET

  • Above the age of 17 years, Passed 10+2 with Physics, Chemistry, Biology as core subjects from a recognized board
  • Class 12 or equivalent appearing aspirants are also eligible to apply for NEET
  • General category aspirants must have passed the qualifying examination with 45% marks, while for SC/ST/OBC the minimum marks should be 40%

Eligibility

Entrance Exam: JEE-Main & Advanced

Passed 10+2 with Physics, Chemistry, Maths as core subjects along with Language and one more subject of choice from a recognized board

Eligibility

Entrance Exam: PCS- Judiciary Prelims

  • The age limit for the UP PCS J Exam is between 22 and 35
  • Lower Judiciary Services-Degree in LLB, enroll as an advocate under the Advocates’ Act 1961 to be eligible for appearing in the Examination
  • Higher Judiciary Services-Graduate in law and have a minimum seven years of litigating practice for appearing for the Higher Judiciary Services Exam

Eligibility

Entrance Exam: Mains

  • Must hold a Graduate Degree from a recognized University
  • Candidate must be a minimum of 21 years of age and must not be more than 32 years of age
