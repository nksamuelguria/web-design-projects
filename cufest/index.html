Welcome to CUFEST 2023

A Little Anecdote

Chandigarh University embarked its legendary journey a few years ago and has now become one of the world’s largest hub for excellent skill development and career preparation. It gained popularity through these years in the context of academics, innovative technology, co-curricular activities, cultural activities and many more. It offers the best platform to the students for the development of their skills and presentation of their talent.

Its commitment to guide the students and help them achieve their goals in a very competent manner has set the standards high and has helped many students attain the best positions in their metiers. It is like a beacon helping the student’s progress towards obtaining the best education and polishing themselves to shine out brightly.

The ladder of success is not easy to climb but also not hard for the one who makes an effort towards climbing it. Set a vision, understand it, try making it true and you will be surprised by the way it suddenly emerges out as something excellent. This is the norm that the University has adopted and is carving high values in the young minds that choose to seek its refuge.

A Glimpse into the CU Fest 2023

Culture is what describes a subject the best. Whether it is about a place, a person or any other subject, the best description can only be given by the customs the subject follows. Chandigarh University organizes the cultural fest every year to bring out the best cultural exchange on the grand platform it provides. Many renowned personalities like the famous Bollywood singer and composer Pritam Chakraborty, Neha Kakkar, the living legend Gurdas Maan-the pride of Punjab, Mithoon and many superstars from Bollywood like Akshay Kumar, Sanjay Dutt, John Abraham, Ranbir Kapoor, Katrina Kaif, Shraddha Kapoor and many more such prominent artists grace the occasion with their valuable presence and add charm to the event with their exuberant proficiency. A remarkable footfall of more than 25000 students is witnessed every year on this prestigious occasion. It helps the young souls explore and discover the talents within them.

And because you have got the talent, we bring you the most adventurous stage for the most breathtaking experience. Join us on the day of CU Fest 8th and 9th of March, 2023 and grab your chance to exhibit all the thrill and passion inside you. It would surely be a beneficent step towards becoming the best version of yourselves.

Installation- Best out of waste

Rules

Installation- Best Out of Waste

Install or create an atmosphere related with the subject or title of the installation. The size of the installation should be 5'x5'x5' maximum.

  • Participants can use all the material, which they use in other art compositions like cartooning, painting, rangoli, poster, collage and clay modelling.

  • Participants are not allowed to use any already composed images or forms available in the market. They should compose or create their own image with the raw material.
  • This is a group activity as four (4) participants can participate but they will create only one installation whose size must not be bigger than 5'x'5'x5'.
  • Time limit is 2 hours and 30 minutes.

Rap Battle

Rules

Rap Battle

  • A minimum of one (1) and a maximum of six (6) people can register for this competition. One submission per MIST team.
  • There will be a separate competition for brothers and for sisters. Hence, each team must either be all male or all female.
  • The performance should not exceed more than 5 minutes. Points will be deducted from the total score if the performance exceeds the allotted time.
  • The only instrument that can be used for the performance is a drum with an open end (daf). Full drum kits, foor drums, etc. will not be allowed.
  • Each group is responsible for bringing their own drums. Other methods used for rhythm may be explored such as vocal percussion (beatboxing), etc.
  • Groups cannot use digitally enhanced sounds for their performance.

01

Debate

Rules

Debate

  • Each participant will be given maximum 10 minutes' time to speak either in favour or against the subject. A warning bell will be given after 8 minutes and a final bell after 10 minutes. If the participants exceed the allocated time than the marks will be deducted at the discretion of the judges.
  • The team supporting the motion must not shift its point of view. The same goes for the opposition, who must oppose the motion completely (whatever their private opinions may be).
  • If a speaker makes a statement, they must be able to provide evidence or reasons to support the statement.
  • Facts presented in a debate must be accurate.
  • Speakers may not bring up new points in a rebuttal speech.

Prizes

  • First Prize: 5,100
  • Second Prize: 4,100
  • Third Prize: 3,100
